About Me

I am a researcher in Environmental Science and Data Science with a strong interest in Machine Learning applications in Ecology. My academic journey has led me to explore the intersections of technology, sustainability, and climate science.

My expertise includes data analysis, modeling, and working with large-scale environmental datasets to extract meaningful insights. I have experience in interdisciplinary research and collaborating with experts from diverse fields.

Beyond research, I am passionate about leveraging computational tools to address pressing environmental challenges and making scientific knowledge more accessible to the broader community.
